At Log Ryan we are comitted to protecting your privacy. The below Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you visit our site or communicate with us through various channels. This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Information Collected

The following types of information may be collected from end users:

  • Website Analytics: Non-personal data about the website usage, such as traffic, sources, behavior, conversions, user device, browser, and geographic location.
  • Personal Information Submitted Voluntarily: When users reach out via forms or other methods, the following information may be collected:
    • User's Name
    • Email Address
    • Phone Number
    • Address
    • Any additional information provided voluntarily

How your information is used

The information you provide is used to:

  • Respond to inquires or provide the services requested
  • Maintain confidential communication about ongoing or future projects.
  • Contact you regarding your project, if necessary.
  • Ensure all data collected through phone or email converations if kept private and confidential.

Confidentiality and Project Information

  • Any information discussed over phone, email, or other communication methods will remain strictly confidential between both parties.
  • No video or photographic material related to the user's project will be captured or shared without explicit consent from the designated project lead on the user's behalf.

Third-Party Sharing

Personal information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties unless required by law or necessary to provide services request by you (such as integrating with project-related services, with your prior knowledge).

Data Security

Considerable measures are taken to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and absolute data security can not be guarenteed in the event of a data breach.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we have collected about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational retention requirments.

Cookies and Analytics

Cookies or tracking tools may be used to analyse website traffic. This information is only collected to understand site usage patterns and improve user experience.
